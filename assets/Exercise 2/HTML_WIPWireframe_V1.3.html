the attentive exercise
What does it mean to listen?
We often try to seperate "listening" from simply "hearing" (audible feedback),
but how do we know if we're doing one and not the other? What are signs
we can pay attention to that express a person's underlying feelings and needs,
rather than only relying on what a person says? And how might we better
work together to communicate these needs? This exercise is an investigation
of these questions in communication.
the attentive exercise
This is how it works:
  1. Watch a short movie clip.
  2. You have 45 seconds to discuss with your partner.
  3. Answer in 1 minute.
